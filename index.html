Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients:


  • 55g chocolate
  • 140g butter
  • 150g sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 100g flour
  • 200g ground almonds
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla sugar

Instructions:


1. Preheat the oven to 180°C fan oven.

2. Melt the butter.

3. Separate the eggs.

4. Whisk the egg whites.

5. Add all the ingredients except the egg whites to the butter and mix.

6. Finally, fold in the egg whites until the batter is well combined but still fluffy.

7. Pour the batter into the greased springform pan and bake for
approx. 30 minutes, test with a skewer.

8. The cake should still be moist but not runny. Leave the cake to cool.
